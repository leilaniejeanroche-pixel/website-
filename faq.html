Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions for students, parents, and guardians.

How do students create an account?

Open Student Portal, fill out the account form, then sign in using the created username and password.

Where can students see their balance?

After login, the student dashboard shows subjects and billing balance.

Who can edit bills?

Only the admin controller can edit student bill records.

Where are announcements posted?

Announcements are shown on the Announcements page and homepage dashboard preview.
