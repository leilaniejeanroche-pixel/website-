Saint Patrick's Academy, Inc.

Saint Patrick's Academy, Inc. is a school community dedicated to guiding learners through quality education, discipline, faith, and service. Set in a peaceful green campus, the school provides a welcoming place where students can grow in knowledge, character, and confidence.

Our School

A place for learning and character formation

The academy supports students in building strong academic foundations while also developing respect, responsibility, and care for others. Teachers, staff, parents, and students work together to create a safe and supportive learning environment.
